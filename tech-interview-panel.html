Interviewer Guidelines

  • Difficulty tags are indicative; probe all candidates for depth regardless of tier.
  • Technical accuracy should be weighted equally with communication clarity for advisory roles.
  • Follow-up probes are strongly encouraged — surface candidates' real-time reasoning, not memorized answers.
  • Candidates who acknowledge the limits of their knowledge and reason from principles score higher than those who confabulate confidence.
  • Questions Q31–Q42 should be treated as the primary evaluation block for senior advisory appointments.
  • Model answers are benchmarks, not scripts — award full marks for alternative well-reasoned responses.
  • This document should be updated quarterly given the pace of AI and quantum developments.
